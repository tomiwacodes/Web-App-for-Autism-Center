ABOUT US:

Autism is not a disability, it is a different ability.

  • OUR MISSION:

    As the first Autism Rehabilitation Center in the Sultanate, our goal is to provide accessible behavioral treatment, therapy, and counseling for those with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) in our community. Additionally, we hope to educate families on detecting early signs of ASD in their children so rehabilitation can start as early as possible.

  • OUR BACKGROUND:

    Located in Muscat al Quaram, the Autism and Rehabilitation Center officially opened in 2015. Since then, the center has expanded to include 41 administrative staff members and several programs dedicated to improving the wellbeing of those diagnosed with ASD.

  • OUR SERVICES:

    Currently, our services include special education, occupational therapy, speech therapy, psychological counseling and behavior modification. With these programs, we hope to work on the difficulties faced by children with ASD and provide them with the skills needed to lead an independent life in various fields.
